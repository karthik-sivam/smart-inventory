## 🎨 Visual Design Guidelines

### Color Scheme
- **Primary**: iOS Blue (#007AFF)
- **Success**: Green (#34C759) for in-stock items
- **Warning**: Orange (#FF9500) for low stock
- **Error**: Red (#FF3B30) for out-of-stock
- **Background**: System backgrounds (light/dark mode)

### Typography
- **Headers**: San Francisco Bold, 28-34pt
- **Body**: San Francisco Regular, 16-17pt
- **Captions**: San Francisco Regular, 12-13pt
- **Overlay Text**: San Francisco Bold, 24pt in white

### Layout
- **Consistent spacing**: 16px margins, 12px padding
- **Card-based design**: Rounded corners (12px), subtle shadows
- **Tab bar**: Standard iOS tab bar with SF Symbols
- **Navigation**: Back buttons, clear hierarchy

### Screenshot Text Overlays
- **Position**: Bottom third of screen
- **Background**: Semi-transparent dark overlay
- **Text**: White, bold, centered
- **Animation**: Fade in effect suggested

## 🔄 Screenshot Variations

### Light Mode vs Dark Mode
- Show 2-3 screenshots in dark mode
- Ensure all text remains readable
- Maintain brand colors consistently

### Populated vs Empty States
- Show populated screens with realistic data
- Demonstrate the app's capabilities
- Use varied, realistic inventory items

### Localization Ready
- Use generic product names that translate well
- Avoid region-specific items
- Keep text minimal and impactful
